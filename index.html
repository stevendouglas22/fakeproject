Founder

Dr. Steven Douglas

After obtining a PhD in bioenginerring, Dr. Douglas decided to form Calgary BIOworks. He hopes his efforts will allow fundamental groups around the world to pursue their own creations without being inhibited by the narrow minds of those around them. Fun fact: His favourite politician is Donald Trump.
